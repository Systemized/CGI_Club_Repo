About Us

The 3D Art and CGI Club at Georgia State University is a space for students to create artwork and animations in 3D Design spaces, develop their skills, and make connections with other 3D digital artists.

Organization Purpose Statement:

The 3D Art and CGI Club at Georgia State University aims to support 3D artists, helping them form a community and network of support for developing their portfolio and career.
